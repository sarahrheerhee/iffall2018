CHEESY
Garlic Bread


PREP TIME

COOK TIME

READY IN

10 min

15 min

25 min





INGREDIENTS

1 loaf of French Bread

1/3 cup of Parmesan Cheese

1/2 cup of Butter, softened

2 tablespoons of garlic powder

1 teaspoon of dried parsley


INSTRUCTIONS

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

2. Cut bread into 1-inch thick slices, without cutting all the way through the loaf.

3. Mix Parmesan cheese, butter, garlic powder, and parsley in a small bowl.

4. Spread mixture between slices of bread and over the top of the loaf.

5. Wrap loaf in aluminum foil, leaving the top partially uncovered.

6. Bake in preheated oven until butter is melted and bread toasted, 15 to 20 minutes.
